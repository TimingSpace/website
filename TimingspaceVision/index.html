Welcome To TimingSpace Vision

TimingSpace vision is one organization to solve vision problem for free. Our duty is to enable machine to see and our dream is achieve that machine see more than human. If you have any problem, question or challange and if you want to find someone to help you, you can turn to us.

Vision is an attrctive and challaneging problem. It is to discover what is in the image and where are they. We have been researching on machine vision for more than one years and have the ability and confidence to solve many vision problem as soon as possible. We are focus on vision detection, vision tracking and vision measurement now.

Detection is find out whether our target is in the image or not and if yes find where is it. It is a sub problem of vision which only cares the target we want to detect and just classify the content of an image into two class: foregroud and backgroud.
